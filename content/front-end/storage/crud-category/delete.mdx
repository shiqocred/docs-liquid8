---
title: "Delete"
---

- Klik menu “Category Setting -> Sub Category”, lalu klik button Delete (icon tong sampah). Akan muncul pop up, “jika” sudah yakin klik button confirm untuk menghapus data. **Penghapusan Data** bersifat **Permanen,** diharapkan penggunaan dengan **Bijak.**
  ![CRUD Category Delete](/image/storage/crud-category/delete/a-1.png "CRUD Category Delete")
  ![CRUD Category Delete](/image/storage/crud-category/delete/a-2.png "CRUD Category Delete")
- Jika sudah terconfirm maka alur **Delete Category** selesai.
