- Buka halaman “Palet -> List Palet” lalu klik button unbundle (Opened Box) untuk melakukan unbundle palet, akan muncul pop up konfirmasi.
  ![Unbundle Palet](/image/storage/palet/unbundle/a-1.png "Unbundle Palet")
  ![Unbundle Palet](/image/storage/palet/unbundle/a-2.png "Unbundle Palet")

- Klik confirm, alur **Delete Palet** selesai.
