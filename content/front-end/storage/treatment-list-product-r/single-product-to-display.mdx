---
title: "Single Product To Display"
---

- Buka halaman “List Product Repair” lalu klik button to display (button paper bag warna biru), akan muncul pop up untuk olah data detail product yang akan dipindah kan ke display. Pada **Old Data** hanya bisa diubah pada data **price** saja, di **New Data** bisa diubah **Semua** inputan keuali pada **Barcode.**
  ![Single Product To Display](/image/storage/treatment-list-product-r/single-product-to-display/a-1.png "Single Product To Display")
  ![Single Product To Display](/image/storage/treatment-list-product-r/single-product-to-display/a-2.png "Single Product To Display")

- Jika sudah semua klik button To Display, alur **SINGLE PRODUCT TO DISPLAY** selesai.
