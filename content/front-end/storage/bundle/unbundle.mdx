---
title: "Unbundle"
---

- Buka halaman “Moving Product->Bundle”, lalu klik button Unbundle. Akan muncul pop up, jika yakin maka klik button confirm. **Penghapusan Data** bersifat **Permanen,** diharapkan penggunaan dengan **Bijak.**  
  ![Unbundle Bundle](/image/storage/bundle/unbundle/a-1.png "Unbundle Bundle")
  ![Unbundle Bundle](/image/storage/bundle/unbundle/a-2.png "Unbundle Bundle")
- Jika sudah selsai dengan klik button **Confirm.** alur **Unbundle Bundle** selesai.
