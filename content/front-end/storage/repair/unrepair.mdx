title: "Unrepair"
---

- Klik menu “Moving Product->Repair” lalu klik button unbundle(icon kardus terbuka), akan muncul pop up dan klik confirm jika sudah sesuai. Barang akan kembali ke display.
  ![Unrepair Repair](/image/storage/repair/unrepair/a-1.png "Unrepair Repair")
  ![Unrepair Repair](/image/storage/repair/unrepair/a-2.png "Unrepair Repair")
- Jika sudah terconfirm unbundle repair, maka alur **Unbundle Repair** selesai.
