title: "Delete"
---

- Masuk kehalaman “Palet -> Warehouse” lalu klik button Delete (icon tong sampah). Akan muncul pop up untuk confirm penghapusan data. **Penghapusan Data** akan membuat data **hilang** secara **Permanen.**  
  ![CRUD Warehouse Delete](/image/storage/crud-warehouse/delete/a-1.png "CRUD Warehouse Delete")
  ![CRUD Warehouse Delete](/image/storage/crud-warehouse/delete/a-2.png "CRUD Warehouse Delete")
- Jika sudah terconfirm maka alur **Delete Warehouse** selesai.
